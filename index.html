About us

CompoundCode is a non-governmental & non-profit organization which aids and Support Kids / children, Students & People of Different communities ( For those who are interested in programming, Software development & Coding), learn computer applicable skills, by providing them with essential tools and a platform that will support and help put to Practice their Ideas, Inversions, Passion and curiosity towards solving problems of our community. CompoundCode offers free computer programming resources and tools for such curious children & People of Various communities. #CompoundCode Coding to solve global & Community Problems.
